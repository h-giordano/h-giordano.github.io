I've lived in Allen for my entire life, but I went between schools growing up, from going to a Montessori school, to a small private school that we later found out was mainly a front for the owner's ponzi scheme, before ending up in the AllenISD school system.
I guess what I learned from having a wide variety of educational experiences is that there were a lot of things that i'd yet to learn, and certain things I needed to unlearn.
As well as having general programming experience for a long time, I got briefly involved in game design and have made some questionably functional games which are now buried somewhere in my old hard drives.
