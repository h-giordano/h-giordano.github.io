I guess what I learned from having a wide variety of educational experiences is that there were a lot of things that i'd yet to learn, and certain things I needed to unlearn.

As well as having general programming experience for a long time, I got briefly involved in game design and have made some questionablly functional games which are now buried somewhere in my old hard drives.

Importance
